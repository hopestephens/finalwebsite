Welcome

Indoor plants should be an essential component of every interior design. Greenery brightens up indoor spaces and are known to have mood-boosting qualities.
Indoor plants are popular because they are relatively easy to take care of. Indoor plants are a great option for those who have little yard space for an outdoor garden or for those who live in climates with severely cold winters.

What qualifies as a houseplant?

A houseplant is, quite simply, a plant that grows indoors. There are a variety of tropical plants, herbs, and vines that especially thrive in indoor environments.

How much experience do I have as a plant owner?

  • Beginner houseplant owners usualy have one year or less of experience. Additionally, if you are struggling to take care of relatively low-maintenance plants, the "tips for beginners" page may be helpful.
  • If you have two to three years of experience growing low-maintenance to average indoor plants, you are an intermediate plant owner!
  • Experienced gardeners shold have grown houseplants for at least four years. The "tips for experiened gardeners" page has advice on how to care for high-maintenance plants.
  • Regardless of your experience level, Here are some tips to help you find plants that have potential to thrive in your space and how you can keep them growing!
